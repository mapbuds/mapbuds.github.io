About Us

Lets Build The Quality Map Of Nepal.

MapBud is a Geotech volunteering organization which focuses on OpenStreetMap datasets and various Geospatial domains. It is founded by 3 pioneers advanced OSM mappers from Nepal with the motive to create digital data. Our Mission is to make Nepal fully digital with every piece of quality data available to everyone.

Our Mission

Create A Digital Nepal.

We are in a mission to create a digital profile of Nepal using freely editable map platform i.e. OpenStreetMap. We would be happy to Collaborate with any organizations, studnets club, NGO'S, INGO'S who are willing to create a build a quality data of Nepal. Our Missions:

Open Data Advocacy

As Open data is the future in tech industry, MapBud will work to create a open data ecosystem in Nepal.

Digital Volunteers

We are in mission of motivating more than 200 volunteers yearly, mostly University level students to contribute in OpenStreetMap.

High Quality Geodata

MapBud aims produce high quality geodata that could be used by local communities, researchers, students, organisations, government and other concerned authorities for data based decision making.
